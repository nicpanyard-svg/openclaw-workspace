# Graham Stock Board

Standalone stock board/app plan for Nick.

Purpose:
- visible board for younger/emerging public companies
- high-upside research funnel, not blind auto-buying
- card-based buy/add/trim/thesis-break guidance
- daily email draft format for `nick.panyard@inetlte.com`

Files:
- `BOARD_SPEC.md` — board structure, scoring, and card fields
- `OPERATING_MODEL.md` — Graham's ownership model and ongoing maintenance cadence
- `CANDIDATES.md` — first-pass research list
- `email-template.md` — daily Graham email format
- `board.seed.json` — starter data model for the board

Guardrails:
- These are research candidates, not final recommendations.
- Younger/emerging does not always mean newly IPO'd; it can also mean earlier-stage public companies still in the scale-up phase.
- High upside usually comes with high drawdown risk. Treat position sizing and thesis-break levels seriously.
